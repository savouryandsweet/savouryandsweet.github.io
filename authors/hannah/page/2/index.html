Hannah

Hannah MacTavish is an experienced Registered Dietitian, avid baker and mom of three. Driven by science and continuous learning, she takes pride in providing the best possible care to her patients. As a registered dietitian, her goals include teaching people how to enjoy a healthy diet and communicating nutrition research in ways that everyone can understand. Hannah has left the clinical world to start her own business, Savoury and Sweet Nutrition, to encourage and support others to build a positive relationship with food.
