The AATT: argula, avocado, tempeh bacon and tomato sandwich

Hannah ,  November 16, 2020

Hi friends!

I love a good sandwich, especially if it has a ton of fillings. Of course, a BLT is one of my favourites, but rarely do I have plain lettuce hanging around. I have tried making tempeh bacon in the past, since we enjoy trying new foods and try to eat vegetarian meals more often than not. Unfortunately, my tempeh bacon was a bit of a flop. I couldn’t cut it thinly enough to get crispy. Lucky for me, I found pre-marinated tempeh at Sprouts this week! I pan fried it in oil and 4/5 of us loved it on this sandwich. We also used some heirloom brown tomatoes called Kumatoes which were tasty.

I recommend sourdough bread for this sandwich, it just adds the perfect tang. Fill your sandwich with a couple slices each of bacon, tomato and avocado and a handful of arugula. Butter or mayo make a great addition as a spread, or even hummus would go well with this sandwich.

Tempeh is a fermented soy product, which means it is full of healthy bacteria…until it’s cooked that is. However, tempeh is full of prebiotics that feed the bacteria in our gut, which supports a healthy digestive system. Tempeh is also high in protein, fibre, B vitamins, calcium, magnesium and zinc.

Tempeh is delicous and so good for you. Give it a try!
